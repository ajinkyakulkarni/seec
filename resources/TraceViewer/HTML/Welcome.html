Welcome to the SeeC Trace Viewer.

SeeC allows you to record the execution of programs that have been written in the C programming language. We call the record of your program's execution a trace. You can open and view these traces using this trace viewer. It will allow you to move forwards and backwards through the recorded execution of your program.

SeeC may have detected runtime errors during your program's execution. These will also be displayed by this trace viewer.
